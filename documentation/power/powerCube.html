Power Cube

The Power Cube is the main form of power storage and transfer within Modular FluxFields

The power cube is the main power storage unit within Modular FluxFields. The power cube is also used to transfer power between other power cubes, as well as machines. Power cubes will evenly distribute their power to other power cubes within a radius of 16 blocks. No tools or cables are required, power cubes automatically link up to each other (You may have to give it up to 5s for existing power cubes to recognize new power cubes). Placing a power cube on top of a machine will allow that machine to draw power in from that power cube. The size of the cube within the spinning power cube frame is an indicator as to how much power is stored within that cube. The bigger it is, the more power that the power cube holds. Also, the cube changes from red to blue as the power level decreases.

Trivia

  • The power cube used to be a power sphere, although this was scrapped due to (huge) lag issues.
