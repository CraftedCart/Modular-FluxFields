Crystal Constructor

The Crystal Constructor is the main crafting mechanic within Modular FluxFields

The crystal constructor is the main machine used to craft items within Modular FluxFields. Like a regular crafting table, you put items into a 3 x 3 grid and an item pops out of the output slot. Unlike a regular crafting table however, the crystal constructor requires power to run, and items take a while to craft.
The crystal refinery requires power to run. Place a Power Cube above it and it will draw power from that.

Processing

The crystal refinery can process the following items.

Alurite Base

2m (120s / 2400t)

Crystal Sheet x4

3m (180s / 3600t)

GUI


Crystal Constructor GUI


The current power status and time remaining until the raw crystal is refined are displayed at the top.
Insert the recipe into the left slots, and the output comes out of the right slot.
Up to 3 stacks of upgrades may be inserted into the right 3 slots on the GUI.

NEI Integration

If NEI is found to be installed, Modular FluxFields will load it's NEI plugin. This includes support for showing Crystal Constructor recipes.
