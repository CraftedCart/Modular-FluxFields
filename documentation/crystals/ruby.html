Ruby

Ruby is a crystal which is needed to craft several items within Modular FluxFields.

Ruby Ore can be found underground in the Overworld at Y: 20 or below. Mining that will yield between 1 - 4 pieces of Raw Ruby (without a fortune enchanted pickaxe). Raw Ruby cannot be used by itself in crafting recipes however; it must be first refined before it becomes useful. Put Raw Ruby into a Crystal Refinery to yield Refined Ruby.
