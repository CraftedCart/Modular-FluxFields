Amethyst

Amethyst is a crystal which is needed to craft several items within Modular FluxFields.

Amethyst Ore can be found underground in the Overworld at Y: 32 or below. Mining that will yield between 1 - 4 pieces of Raw Amethyst (without a fortune enchanted pickaxe). Raw Amethyst cannot be used by itself in crafting recipes however; it must be first refined before it becomes useful. Put Raw Amethyst into a Crystal Refinery to yield Refined Amethyst.
