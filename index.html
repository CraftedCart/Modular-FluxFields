Modular FluxFields

Forcefields to protect your Minecraft base

Download now!

Modular FluxFields is a Minecraft mod which adds in forcefields into the game.

Primarily designed for use on a server, Modular FluxFields allows players to protect their base from griefing, players entering, and more!

Impenetrable Defenses

Forcefields are impossible to bypass without proper privileges (without being in creative mode of course)

Customisable Permissions

You control who can do what

Custom power system

Redstone Flux didn't exist for 1.8 when I started making this mod
